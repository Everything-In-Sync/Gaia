Weather is the day-to-day state of the atmosphere and its short-term variation in minutes to weeks.
Weather is the combination of temperature, humidity, precipitation, cloudiness, visibility, and wind.

Climate is the weather of a place averaged over a period of time, often 30 years.
Climate information includes the statistical weather information that tells you about the normal weather.
